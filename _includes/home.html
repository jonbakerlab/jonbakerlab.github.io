Microbiology & Bioinformatics to Understand the Oral Microbiome

We investigate how bacteria and viruses in the mouth shape human health—combining long‑read sequencing, mass spectrometry, and computational biology to discover new species, pathways, and mechanisms of disease.

View Publications Contact

What we do

  • Oral microbiome ecology — community structure, function, and dynamics
  • Virome–bacteriome interactions — phage and viral drivers of dysbiosis
  • Long‑read & multi‑omics — nanopore, RNA, and metabolomics pipelines

Highlights

  • Discovered new bacterial species and biosynthetic pathways with implications for oral and systemic disease.
  • Pioneered long‑read approaches for saliva and oral bacterial RNA.
